Hi, I'm Frankie!

This is just a fun introduction about who I am, some fun facts about me and my most popular works. Then I can go a little into my reasons for writing, probably some things about how I love coming up wiht my own little worlds when I was a kid and writing wtories for fun, with some little jokes about annoying siblings that most children will relate to and even a very specific reference to having fun with something that isn't really a thing anymore that parents who grew up in my generation will relate to. Pixel Chix was so sassy!

I can throw in something about how I got the idea for my first book, like maybe it's been something I've had in my head for practically my entire life and I finally got the push to share it in book form, or may the idea just struck me out of nowhere and I knew I just had to make that book. I might have had difficulties finding someone who would publish me until I finally found the right people who were willing to give me a chance, and next thing you know, my mom is calling me to tell me kids are reading my book in the kindergarten she teaches!

Then I conclude by talking about how nowadays I spend my time working on my ideas at home with my delightful spouse and our cats who each have names that were popular in the 30s and 40s, like Dorothy and Nelson. I take a moment to express my gratitude for the privelege of getting to make a career out of doing something I love and how excited I am to keep pumping out titles that technically count as chapter books so that fourth graders can continue to have a fun but relatively easy read they can lean on for filling out their daily reading logs.
